# Privacy Policy

**Concept Pet**
Last updated: March 11, 2026

## Overview

Concept Pet is designed with your privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers or cloud services.

## Data Collection

Concept Pet does **not** collect any personal data. Specifically:

- No analytics or tracking
- No user accounts or sign-in
- No network requests to external servers
- No advertising or ad-related tracking
- No cookies or web-based tracking

## Data Storage

All app data is stored **locally on your device only**, including:

- Game progress (training data, scores, Q-table state)
- App settings and preferences
- Custom pet images

This data never leaves your device. There is no cloud sync, no server-side storage, and no backup to any external service beyond your own device backups (e.g., iCloud device backups managed by Apple under their own privacy policy).

## Apple Intelligence & Image Playground

Concept Pet uses Apple's Image Playground feature to let you generate a custom pet avatar from a photo. This feature is powered by **Apple Intelligence**, which processes data **on-device**. Concept Pet does not access, store, or transmit any data related to this processing. Apple's own privacy policy governs the use of Apple Intelligence features. For more information, see [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## Third-Party Services

Concept Pet does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

Concept Pet does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
